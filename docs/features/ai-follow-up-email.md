# AI Follow-Up Email for Salesforce

This page documents the current public DealScope page for AI follow-up email workflows in Salesforce.

## Why This Matters

The public page frames the follow-up email as the useful output after the brief.

The idea is simple:

- the rep opens the record
- DealScope builds the brief
- the next email comes from the same context

That makes the workflow narrower and more operational than a generic chat response.

## What Context the Draft Needs

The current public page says a useful draft needs more than fields alone.

It highlights the importance of:

- recent activity
- open concerns
- buying signals
- timeline details
- enough record history to avoid a generic draft

## How DealScope Positions the Workflow

The public page positions DealScope as the workflow where the same context powers both:

- the record brief
- the draft follow-up email

It also links the quality of the draft to richer context sources such as:

- LinkedIn messages
- HubSpot activity
- Twilio SMS
- voice notes
