# Twilio-powered SMS in Salesforce

This page documents the current public DealScope page for Twilio and Salesforce integration.

## What the Public Page Promises

The public page positions DealScope as a way to bring Twilio SMS conversations into Salesforce so they become usable deal context.

Its headline promise is that SMS should not stay outside the CRM.

The public page says Twilio conversation history can become:

- activity timeline entries
- part of the AI summary
- source context for signals and insights
- source context for draft emails

## Public Workflow

The current public page shows a simple sequence:

1. Connect Twilio.
2. SMS appears in the Salesforce activity timeline.
3. DealScope analyzes the full conversation.
4. The user gets summary, signals, and draft email.

## Why This Workflow Exists

The public positioning is built around one practical problem:

- key customer details are often shared over SMS
- those details do not reliably make it into Salesforce
- missing message context weakens the brief and the next follow-up
