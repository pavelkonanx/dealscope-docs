# Salesforce Account Summary with DealScope

DealScope has a public Account-specific workflow for turning an Account record into a short AI brief.

## What It Is

The public site describes the Account workflow as a way to understand an Account in seconds.

It highlights:

- short summary
- customer signals
- risks
- stakeholders
- draft follow-up email

## Why Account Summaries Matter

The public page says Account context is usually spread across:

- notes
- activities
- emails
- open opportunities
- support history

That makes it slow to understand the real state of the customer relationship before a call or follow-up.

## What DealScope Generates on an Account

The public page lists:

- `TL;DR summary`
  Short overview of the customer relationship and current context.
- `Buying signals`
  Expansion, repeat order intent, urgency, product interest, or stakeholder engagement.
- `Risks`
  Delivery issues, stalled communication, missing decision context, support friction, or account health concerns.
- `Draft follow-up email`
  A ready-to-edit next email based on the Account context.

## Common Account Use Cases

The public Account page says this is useful when:

- an account manager prepares for a customer call
- a teammate takes over an existing account
- a manager wants a fast overview of account status
- the team wants to spot expansion opportunities
- someone needs to write the next customer email quickly

## Practical Boundary

The public site does not present this as a full customer success platform. It presents it as a faster way to understand the Account record that already exists in Salesforce.
