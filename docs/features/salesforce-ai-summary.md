# Salesforce AI Summary for Leads and Opportunities

This page documents the public DealScope workflow for AI summaries on Lead and Opportunity records.

## What It Does

The public site positions DealScope as a way to understand a record in seconds and act on it without leaving Salesforce.

On Lead and Opportunity records, the public pages say DealScope generates:

- TL;DR summary
- buyer signals
- risks
- draft follow-up email

The home page also includes stakeholders as a core output.

## Why Teams Use It

The public site describes the same problem across Leads and Opportunities:

- useful context is buried across notes, fields, status changes, and activity history
- users need a fast understanding before the next reply
- weak context leads to weak follow-up

DealScope is positioned as the focused workflow for solving that problem.

## Who It Is For

The public site names these audiences:

- Salesforce admins
- Salesforce consultants
- small sales teams
- RevOps teams

## Public Product Notes

The current public pages say this workflow:

- works inside Salesforce record pages
- does not require an Einstein add-on
- is designed for practical record work rather than generic chat

The same public page also says DealScope uses a controlled processing flow and can support excluding selected personal data before AI processing.

## Base Workflow

1. Open a Lead or Opportunity.
2. Click `Generate` in the DealScope panel.
3. Review the brief.
4. Use the draft email as a starting point.
