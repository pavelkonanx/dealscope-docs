# Voice to CRM in Salesforce

This page documents the current public DealScope page for voice-to-CRM activity logging in Salesforce.

## What the Public Page Promises

The current public page positions DealScope as a way to log Salesforce activities using voice instead of typing.

The public description says DealScope can:

- convert speech into a clean activity log
- update the AI summary
- generate a follow-up email from the new context

## Public Workflow

The page shows this sequence:

1. Hold the button and speak your notes.
2. AI converts them into structured activity.
3. Summary and draft email are generated instantly.

## Why This Workflow Matters

The current public page frames this around a common CRM problem:

- reps do not log activities consistently
- notes stay messy or get skipped
- the record loses useful context

The public promise is that voice capture reduces friction and keeps the CRM timeline more complete.

## What the User Gets Instantly

The page explicitly lists:

- clean activity log
- updated AI summary
- buying signals and risks
- draft follow-up email
