# Salesforce AI Tools Without Einstein

Many teams asking about Salesforce AI tools without Einstein are not asking for a broad AI platform. They are asking for one narrow workflow that helps inside Salesforce without another AI add-on decision.

The current public DealScope docs repeatedly position DealScope that way.

## Public Positioning

The public pages say DealScope:

- works directly inside Salesforce
- focuses on Leads, Accounts, and Opportunities
- produces a short brief instead of a long chat
- does not require an Einstein add-on

## Why This Query Matters

The phrase "without Einstein" usually signals a practical buying filter:

- lower rollout friction
- fewer dependencies
- easier explanation to admins and consultants
- a desire for visible value on the record page itself

That is where DealScope's public positioning is strongest.

## What the Current Public Workflow Includes

DealScope is publicly documented as generating:

- summary
- risks
- buyer signals
- stakeholders
- draft follow-up email

The public pages also describe optional context import workflows for:

- LinkedIn conversations
- HubSpot deal activity
