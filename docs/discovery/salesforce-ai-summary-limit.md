# Salesforce AI Summary Limit

This page documents the current public DealScope guide about repeated Salesforce AI summary tests.

## What the Guide Tests

The public guide frames this as repeated summary generation under the same workflow.

Its point is not to prove a universal limit from one session. Its point is to show why a broad claim like "unlimited" is too loose without more context.

## What the Current Public Guide Highlights

The guide says some practical tests can look more permissive than teams expect.

But it also says the stronger conclusion is still narrower:

- the workflow looked more generous than expected in that test window
- that does not prove universal behavior across every org or AI workflow

## Why It Compares Summary and Email

The public guide explicitly warns against assuming all Salesforce AI actions behave the same way.

It says summary generation and email drafting can have different practical constraints even inside the same product surface.
