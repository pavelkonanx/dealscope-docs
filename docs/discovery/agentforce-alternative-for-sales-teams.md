# Agentforce Alternative for Sales Teams

If you are looking for an Agentforce alternative for sales teams, the first question is whether you need a broad native AI layer or a focused record workflow.

The current public DealScope docs position DealScope as the narrower path.

## When a Sales Team Usually Wants an Alternative

Based on the public comparison pages, teams usually look for an alternative when they want:

- a shorter record brief
- clearer risks and buyer signals
- a usable follow-up email from the same context
- less repeated clicking and waiting

That is a more specific need than "Salesforce AI" in general.

## How DealScope Is Publicly Positioned

The public DealScope docs describe a workflow where the user:

1. opens a Lead, Account, or Opportunity
2. generates the brief on the record page
3. reads risks and buyer signals
4. uses the draft follow-up email

This makes the tool easier to place as a sales workflow layer instead of a general AI assistant.

## When Native Salesforce AI May Still Be the Better Fit

Based on the same public materials, Salesforce native AI may still be the better fit when:

- the team wants a broader built-in AI footprint
- a general summary is enough
- the preference is to stay fully within the native Salesforce AI path
