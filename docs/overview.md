# DealScope Overview

DealScope is an AI brief for Salesforce records. It is designed to give a user instant context on a record page without forcing them to read the full activity history first.

The public site describes DealScope as working on:

- Lead
- Account
- Opportunity

## What DealScope Generates

DealScope is positioned around five outputs:

- `TL;DR`
  A short summary of the record and what matters now.
- `Risk flags`
  Warnings and blockers pulled from record context.
- `Buyer signals`
  Signs of intent, urgency, momentum, or expansion.
- `Stakeholders`
  A role-based view of who matters in the deal or account.
- `Draft follow-up email`
  A ready-to-edit email based on the record context.

## What Problem It Solves

The public site describes the same pattern across Leads, Accounts, and Opportunities:

- useful context is scattered across activities, emails, notes, and status changes
- users need a fast understanding before they reply or review the record
- generic AI output is often too broad for a repeatable CRM workflow

DealScope is positioned as the narrow workflow layer that solves that problem on the record page itself.
