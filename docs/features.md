# DealScope Features and Outputs

DealScope is centered on a small set of repeatable outputs. The goal is not to replace Salesforce with another workspace. The goal is to make the current record easier to understand and easier to act on.

## Core Outputs

### TL;DR

A short brief that explains the current state of the record in seconds.

### Risk Flags

Warnings and blockers pulled from activity, notes, and other available context.

### Buyer Signals

Positive intent, urgency, budget interest, momentum, or expansion signs.

### Stakeholders

A role-based view of who influences the decision and how engaged they are.

### Draft Follow-Up Email

A ready-to-edit email generated from the same record context as the brief.

## Product Boundaries

The public site does not position DealScope as:

- a generic chat assistant
- a full CRM replacement
- a full HubSpot and Salesforce sync engine

Its public positioning is narrower: structured record understanding and follow-up support inside Salesforce.
