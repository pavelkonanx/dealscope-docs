# DealScope vs Salesforce Einstein Summaries

This page is a workflow comparison based on the current public DealScope site. It is not a claim that one product replaces the other for every Salesforce use case.

## When Teams Usually Look for an Alternative

The public DealScope site says some teams want an alternative because they:

- do not want extra AI add-ons
- need something simpler
- want value directly on the record page
- care more about a short brief and a usable email draft than about a broad AI layer

## Where DealScope Fits

The public DealScope positioning is narrow and practical:

- short AI brief for the current record
- clear risks and buyer signals
- role-based stakeholder section
- ready-to-edit follow-up email
- workflow centered on Lead and Opportunity pages

## Where Salesforce Native AI May Still Be the Better Fit

Based on the same public positioning, Salesforce native AI may be the better fit when:

- you want the broader Salesforce AI platform
- you prefer native tooling even if the output is more general
- a basic summary is enough for your team

## Practical Difference in Public Positioning

The DealScope public site frames the difference like this:

- Salesforce native AI is a strong general starting point inside CRM
- DealScope is optimized for one focused record workflow
