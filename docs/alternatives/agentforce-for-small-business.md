# DealScope and Agentforce for Small Business

This page summarizes the current public DealScope comparison page for Agentforce for Small Business.

## Public Site Positioning

The public DealScope page says Salesforce now includes AI features in:

- Free Suite
- Starter Suite
- Pro Suite

It also says Starter and Pro add conversational AI through the Employee Agent.

## Where Salesforce Native AI Looks Strong

The public DealScope page presents these strengths for the native Salesforce path:

- built into Salesforce
- easy starting point for small teams
- good high-level overview
- strong market validation for SMB teams adopting AI in CRM

## Where DealScope Positions Itself Differently

The same public page says DealScope fits when teams want:

- a shorter, more action-ready record brief
- clearer risks and buyer signals
- a better next email workflow
- less repeated waiting and clicking
- the brief available directly on the record as part of the team workflow

## Notes from the Current Public Comparison Page

The current public DealScope page says that, in its own testing:

- native summaries may need to be generated on demand
- summary behavior can differ based on what record data is used
- activity counts may be less useful than the actual content of calls and emails

These are public comparison claims from the DealScope site, not independent product certification.

## When DealScope May Be a Better Fit

Based on the public comparison page, DealScope is a better fit when:

- the team works mostly from Leads, Accounts, and Opportunities
- the team wants the same brief visible to multiple users
- the main goal is fast understanding and fast next-step email drafting
