# DealScope Use Cases

These use cases are based on the current public product pages and their examples.

## Fast Handoff Before a Reply

A rep or admin opens a Lead or Opportunity and needs to understand what happened before sending the next email.

DealScope helps by showing:

- a short brief first
- current risks
- buyer signals
- a draft follow-up email

Related page:

- [Salesforce AI summary](./features/salesforce-ai-summary.md)

## Manager Review

A manager needs a fast read on a record without reading the full activity history.

DealScope helps by giving one structured view of:

- current state
- blockers
- momentum
- stakeholder picture

This is useful on both sales records and account records.

## Rep Context Before the Next Follow-Up

The public site repeatedly frames this as a core workflow:

- open the record
- generate the brief
- use the draft email as a starting point

This is useful when a user needs context quickly and does not want to scan the full timeline first.

## Summarizing Account History

On Account records, the public site positions DealScope as a way to summarize ongoing customer history before:

- a customer call
- a handoff to a teammate
- an account review
- an expansion conversation

Related page:

- [Salesforce Account summary](./features/account-summary.md)

## Capturing LinkedIn Conversations in CRM

Some teams work heavily in LinkedIn but still need the CRM timeline to stay complete.

The public LinkedIn import page positions DealScope as a way to:

- paste a full conversation into Salesforce
- split it into dated activity events
- include the imported context in the AI brief

Related page:

- [LinkedIn to Salesforce import](./features/linkedin-to-salesforce.md)

## Bringing HubSpot Deal Context into Salesforce

Some teams keep early deal history in HubSpot and later-stage work in Salesforce.

The public HubSpot page positions DealScope as a way to:

- import deal activity from HubSpot into Salesforce
- improve the AI brief on the Opportunity
- generate a better follow-up email from richer context

Related page:

- [HubSpot to Salesforce import](./features/hubspot-to-salesforce.md)

## Bringing Twilio SMS into the Salesforce Timeline

Some deals move fastest over text, but that context never makes it back into CRM.

The current public Twilio page positions DealScope as a way to:

- bring Twilio conversations into Salesforce activity history
- include SMS in the AI summary
- use message context for signals and draft emails

Related page:

- [Twilio-powered SMS in Salesforce](./features/twilio-salesforce-integration.md)

## Logging Voice Notes Without Typing

Some reps never write clean notes after a call because the logging step takes too long.

The current public voice page positions DealScope as a way to:

- capture spoken notes directly in Salesforce
- convert them into structured activity
- refresh the summary and draft email instantly
