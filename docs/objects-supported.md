# Supported Salesforce Objects

Based on the current public site, DealScope supports the following Salesforce objects.

## Lead

Lead is one of the core public workflows for DealScope.

Public site support includes:

- AI brief generation on the record page
- TL;DR, risks, buyer signals, stakeholders, and draft email
- LinkedIn conversation import on Lead records

Related page:

- [Salesforce AI summary](./features/salesforce-ai-summary.md)

## Account

Account is publicly presented as a first-class DealScope workflow.

Public site support includes:

- AI brief on the Account page
- customer signals and risks
- account-level context before a call or follow-up
- draft follow-up email

Related page:

- [Salesforce Account summary](./features/account-summary.md)

## Opportunity

Opportunity is a core public workflow and also the object used in the HubSpot import page.

Public site support includes:

- AI brief generation on the record page
- TL;DR, risks, buyer signals, stakeholders, and draft email
- LinkedIn conversation import on Opportunity records
- HubSpot activity import for better record context

Related pages:

- [Salesforce AI summary](./features/salesforce-ai-summary.md)
- [HubSpot to Salesforce import](./features/hubspot-to-salesforce.md)
